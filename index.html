Salt Lake City Devs

Salt Lake City Developers exists for anyone interested in or practicing software development, programming & general tech geekiness. We are a language, skill, and platform agnostic community built to promote the learning and development of the greater salt lake area's tech community, and having a fun time doing it.

Whether you're an expert, or just getting started, we hope you'll join us.
